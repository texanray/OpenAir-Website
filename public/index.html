The Problem

Locked into expensive ecosystems?

Most IoT providers force you to buy their proprietary hardware, their sensors, their gateways — locking you into their system forever. When you want to scale or switch, you're stuck. And the costs keep climbing.

Our Solution

Your hardware. Our connectivity.

We don't sell you equipment — we connect what you already have. Buy off-the-shelf sensors. Use your existing infrastructure. We handle the data layer: collection, transmission, storage, and visualization. You stay in control.
